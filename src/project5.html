HUNGRY STINGRAY

Hungry Stingray

Experience the thrill of an underwater adventure in the Stingray Attack Game. Control a Stingray, survive, and collect food while avoiding poisonous objects. Dive into dynamic gameplay with increasing difficulty waves and aim for the highest score.

Key Features

Navigate the Stingray: Control the Stingray through the underwater world.

Collect Food: Gather different types of food for points.

Avoid Poisonous Objects: Stay alive by steering clear of poisonous objects.

Dynamic Gameplay: Experience increasing difficulty waves for a challenging adventure.

Score Tracking: Keep track of your score and aim for the top score.
